Welcome to China

China, one of the world’s oldest civilizations, is a vast country filled with rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking landscapes. From the Great Wall to modern megacities like Beijing and Shanghai, China offers a fascinating blend of the ancient and the futuristic.

Top Attractions

  • The Great Wall of China
  • Forbidden City in Beijing
  • Terracotta Army in Xi'an
  • Li River in Guilin
  • Shanghai skyline and The Bund

Famous Foods

  • Peking Duck – crispy roasted duck
  • Dim Sum – small Cantonese dishes
  • Kung Pao Chicken – spicy stir-fried chicken
  • Dumplings – filled with meat or vegetables
  • Sweet and Sour Pork

Practice Questions

Q1: What is the most famous landmark in China?

A1: The Great Wall of China.

Q2: What is a traditional Chinese dish made from roasted duck?

A2: Peking Duck.

Q3: Where is the Terracotta Army located?

A3: In Xi'an.

Q4: What is the capital of China?

A4: Beijing.
