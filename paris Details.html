Paris, France

Description: Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light". It is famous for its romantic ambiance, artistic culture, and rich history.

Main Attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Champs-Élysées, and Montmartre.

Famous Foods: Croissants, Baguettes, Macarons, Crêpes, and French Cheese.

Fun Facts: Paris has more than 1,800 bakeries and is home to over 130 museums.
