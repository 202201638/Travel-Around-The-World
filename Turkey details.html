Welcome to Turkey

Turkey is a country that bridges Europe and Asia, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes. From the bustling streets of Istanbul to the fairy chimneys of Cappadocia, every corner of Turkey offers something unique.

Top Attractions

  • Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque in Istanbul
  • Ancient city of Ephesus
  • Hot air balloons over Cappadocia
  • Pamukkale’s white travertine terraces
  • Bosphorus cruises and Turkish bazaars

Famous Foods

  • Kebab (doner, shish)
  • Baklava – sweet pastry with nuts
  • Turkish delight (lokum)
  • Meze – appetizers with hummus, eggplant, and more
  • Turkish tea and strong coffee

Practice Questions

Q1: What are two famous mosques in Istanbul?

A1: Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque.

Q2: What sweet is Turkey famous for?

A2: Baklava and Turkish delight.

Q3: What is a popular drink in Turkey?

A3: Turkish tea and Turkish coffee.

Q4: What is special about Cappadocia?

A4: It has fairy chimneys and hot air balloon rides.
