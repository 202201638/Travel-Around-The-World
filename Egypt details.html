Welcome to Egypt

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders and timeless beauty. It's home to one of the world’s oldest civilizations, with awe-inspiring monuments like the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx. From desert adventures to Nile cruises, Egypt is filled with unforgettable experiences.

Top Attractions

  • The Great Pyramids and Sphinx in Giza
  • Valley of the Kings in Luxor
  • Karnak and Abu Simbel temples
  • The Egyptian Museum in Cairo
  • Nile River cruises and Red Sea beaches

Famous Foods

  • Koshari – rice, pasta, lentils with tomato sauce
  • Ful Medames – cooked fava beans
  • Molokhia – green soup with garlic
  • Taameya (Egyptian falafel)
  • Basbousa and Kunafa for dessert

Practice Questions

Q1: What are the most famous landmarks in Egypt?

A1: The Great Pyramids and the Sphinx.

A2: Koshari.

A3: The Egyptian Museum.
