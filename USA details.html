Discover the United States of America

Why Visit the USA?

The USA is a vast and diverse country offering a mix of natural wonders, modern cities, cultural landmarks, and exciting entertainment. From the Grand Canyon to Times Square, and from Yellowstone to Disney World, there's something for everyone.

Famous Landmarks:

  • Statue of Liberty
  • Grand Canyon
  • Golden Gate Bridge
  • Times Square
  • Hollywood Sign

Popular Foods:

  • Cheeseburgers and Fries
  • Hot Dogs
  • Mac and Cheese
  • Apple Pie
  • Fried Chicken

English Practice - Questions & Answers

Q1: What is the capital of the USA?
A: Washington, D.C.

Q2: What is the Statue of Liberty?
A: It is a famous symbol of freedom located in New York City.

Q3: Name one famous American food.
A: Hamburger.

Q4: Why do people visit the USA?
A: To see famous landmarks, enjoy theme parks, and experience the culture.
